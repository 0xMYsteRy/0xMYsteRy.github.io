Sigma Male - The Secret Society Behind The Wall

0x01 Sigma male - The man behind the scene

  • It is not so supprised that most people are unaware of the sigma male.
  • They are the one who stay behind the scenes, never show off and work quietly to make things happen.
  • Sigma males rarely get credits for their contribution since they are not looking for it or do not feel the need to care about it.
  • It is only when something goes wrong someone might notice them. When everything going smoothly, they are just in the background doing what needs to be done without complaint or fanfare.
  • Yet no one knows who these quite heroes really are until disaster strike and silence becomes betrayal.

0x02 Sigma male are typically highly intelligent

  • They work hard not to be seen as nerds or geeks.
  • They have great observational skills.
  • Many sigma male children are bullied in school because of their intelligence so they learn at an early age how to blend in socially with little effort.

0x03 Sigma male don’t want to stand out from the crowd

  • This does not mean they are lack of confidence when dealing with socializing. Sigma male does not like small talk and often preceived as being simple minded by those who do not understand them. This is a perfect facade for the enormous capabilities for concentration on the task at hand.
  • Sigma males like work and they love to learn. They typically have high IQs and excel in technology, finance and engineering. Many of them are also Nomads, however, their ability to stay anonymity and stay hidden allows them to be effective involve in almost industry or profession.
  • Sigma males tend to avoid the celebrity spotlight that comes with career archivement while quietly accummulating more power and wealth than most people could ever imagine.

0x04 Sigma male loves to learn in solitude

  • They read, study and acquire as much knowledge as possible.
  • They are the ultimate knowledge workers interested in understanding how things work at a very deep level. As such, they often become expert in their chosen fields and may teach other what they knows.
  • There is a wide misconception that all smart people work alone but some of these individuals prefer to band together with like-minded individuals who share similar ideas and goals. They like to work alone but when the goal is too big they look for each other to form a team and archive their goal. Such groups gather where there is little chance of being noticed such as basements, libraries or any place out of public eye.

0x05 Sigma men resolve conflicts with logic

  • They rely on direct communication to confront the problem. This is also the reason why Sigma men want to operate behind the scenes as they rarely want conflict unless it’s absolutely neccessary.
